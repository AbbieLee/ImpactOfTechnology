TECHWORLD was established to educate the public on the influences that
technology have on us and our world. Within the last couple decades, the
way in which technology is intertwined with everyday life has grown to an
unprecedented amount, and our goal is to bring the awareness to the topical
issues that we currently face. Whilst technology may enhance our lives in
many ways, we believe that conscious consumption is key. Join us in our
journey to creating a better tech world!
